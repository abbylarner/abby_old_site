SOME OF MY CREATIONS:

  • Design Like Mad Website

    This website is for an event that I organized in Madison to bring together student and professional designers to solve design problems for local non-profit organizations over a 12-hour period. Design like mad is now in 4 cities across the US and growing!

  • 900dpi.com

    900dpi is a simple hosted CMS for web designers. I'm the co-founder and head designer for 900dpi.

  • 900dpi Logo

    I am in charge of all of the branding for 900dpi. This is the logo I came up with to represent the fun, clean, and simple aspects of our app.

  • CrossFit Madtown Website

    This project was a redesign of the CrossFit Madtown website built on the 900dpi platform. The owners wanted a simplified website that allowed their customers to easily find the information they were looking for.

  • RAZ Productions Logo

    I created this logo for RAZ Productions, a startup that focuses on helping up and coming musicians perform and build an audience.

  • RAZ Productions Website

    Website design and development for RAZ Productions.

  • Solstice Mobile App

    I designed this app for the Solstice Music Festival that happens each year on the summer solstice and sponsors "pop-up" concerts throughout the city. They were in need of an interface that would allow concert goers to see what concerts where playing at what times around the city.

  • Sasquash T-Shirt

    A T-Shirt design for my squash team, "Team Sasquash."

  • Scrooge Buster Mobile App

    This app allows users to share holiday wish lists. I designed and developed this app with node.js.

  • Zing Coaching Logo

    I created the logo and branding for Zing Coaching. It was important to keep the logo clean, modern, and fun.

  • Cville Rail Logo

    This logo was created in collaboration with two other designers at the Charlottesville Design Marathon in 2009. Cville rail was looking for a modern logo and marketing materials to move their company forward. They also wanted to incorporate the regional aspect (with the blue ridge mountains).
